About AquaInk



AquaInk's inception was only a matter of time. In 2007, founder Joel Higham started a small IT business in San Luis Obispo CA which eventually transformed into a Web Design company called WebForge. After many years of building websites mostly as a solo enterprise, Joel realized that it was time to expand the business into something bigger.

The past several years were spent building custom web pages that performed specific functions. Eventually projects would come along that were grand in scope, and a new business had to be created to fit those needs. Top talent was pooled together to create a team that could build new and interesting applications that were fun to use, and easy to navigate.

AquaInk was essentially created at this point, and today strives to be the tip of the spear in web applications. We wear a lot of hats here at Aqua Ink, so feel free to challenge us and task us with hard projects. It would be our honor to help you tackle them.

Thanks for stopping by!
